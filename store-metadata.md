## Description

Cat Toy Box is the ultimate interactive game collection designed for your cat! Watch your furry friend pounce, swipe, and chase across 6 exciting game modes.

**FREE MODES:**
🐛 Bug – A speedy critter scurries across the screen
🔴 Laser – The classic red dot your cat can't resist

**PREMIUM MODES:**
🐟 Fish – Colorful fish swim across the display
✨ Sparkle – Mesmerizing floating lights to chase
🐞 Ladybug – Cute ladybugs that fly and crawl
🐍 Snake – Wiggly snakes slither around the screen

**FEATURES:**
• Realistic animations powered by Skia graphics engine
• Combo tracking – see how many hits your cat chains together
• Session stats – track hits, combos, and play time
• Cat-safe back button (3-second hold to exit)
• Beautiful themed backgrounds for each mode
• No distracting UI – designed for paws, not fingers

**Cat Toy Box Pro** unlocks all 6 modes, removes ads, and includes all future game modes!

Safe, fun, and endlessly entertaining for cats of all ages.
