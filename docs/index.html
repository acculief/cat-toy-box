Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Cat Toy Box ("we", "our", or "the app") is an interactive game app designed for cats. We take your privacy seriously.

Information We Collect

Cat Toy Box does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation or login.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat – to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through Apple's App Store. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Game scores and preferences are stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to external servers by the app itself.

Advertising

Free users may see interstitial ads provided by Google AdMob. Premium users do not see any ads. You may be asked for permission to track your activity across apps for personalized ads (App Tracking Transparency).

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. It is designed for cats and their adult owners.
